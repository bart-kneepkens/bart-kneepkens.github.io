Hi there. My name is Bart Kneepkens,

and I'm a developer. My philosophy when it comes to making software reflects in this website; minimal, concise, and to the point.


"Nothing is more simple than greatness; indeed, to be simple is to be great."

- Waldo Emerson

Creating good experiences with software is about more than conforming to a person's or business' needs or expectations; it's about going beyond that. Instead of making things that add to life, we should aim to make things that greatly improve life.

To make things truly simplistic and truly good, is to make things as if they are what we have always missed.
