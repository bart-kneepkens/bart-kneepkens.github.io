Hi there. My name is Bart Kneepkens,

and I'm a developer. My philosophy when it comes to making software reflects in this website; minimal, concise, and to the point.


"Nothing is more simple than greatness; indeed, to be simple is to be great."

- Waldo Emerson

Simplicity is the one thing I value more than well-structured code and clean desks. When writing software, the question I keep asking myself is; What would a typical user expect from this? That's the starting point.

Making an experience is about more than conforming to the person's needs or expectations; it's about going beyond that. Instead of making something that adds to life, I like to make things that extend our lives. To make something truly simplistic, is to make something as if it is what we have always missed.
